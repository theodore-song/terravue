How it works

01

Pull the data

Daily prices for roughly 1,000 US stocks plus major ETFs from Yahoo Finance.

02

Score every name

Four technical strategies blend into one conviction score and a BUY / HOLD / SELL call per stock.

03

Five agents trade

Each agent ranks the universe its own way and rebalances a paper portfolio. Click any ticker for a full profile.

04

Compete daily

The leaderboard tracks who is winning after each weekday run.

Terravue is an educational paper-trading simulation on delayed market data. It places no real orders and is not financial advice.
