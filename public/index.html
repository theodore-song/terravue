How it works

01

Pull the data

Daily prices for the top ~1,000 US stocks (S&P 500 / 400 / 600) plus major ETFs from Yahoo Finance.

02

Score every name

Four technical strategies blend into one conviction score and a BUY / HOLD / SELL call per stock.

03

Five agents trade

Each agent ranks the names its own way and rebalances its own paper portfolio — click any ticker for a full profile.

04

Compete daily

They run automatically every weekday after the close (in the cloud); the leaderboard tracks who's winning.

Terravue is an educational paper-trading simulation on delayed market data. It places no real orders and is not financial advice.
